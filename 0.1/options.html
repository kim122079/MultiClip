MultiClip Options


Choose MultiClipboard size in numbers [0-50].


If checked, a screenshot of the website text was clipped from will be shown next to the clipped text.


If checked, a dialog will be shown next to the selected text. If the dialog is clicked, the currently selected text is clipped to MultiClipboard and also to the browser clipboard.


If checked, status messages, i.e. when text is clipped to MultiClipboard, or copied to the browser clipboard, will be shown below the omnibox.


Usage:

  • Select any text on any website and press Ctrl+Shift+C to copy text to your MultiClipboard
  • If "Show dialog on releasing mouse key" is checked a small dialog will be shown, when you selected your text. Click the dialog to copy text to your MultiClipboard
  • Text that is copied to your clipboard is also copied to your browser clipboard, like if you would normally use Strg+C
  • Your MultiClipboard is located to the right of your omnibox. Open your MultiClipboard by clicking on the extension icon
  • On your MultiClipboard you can copy the selected text to the browser clipboard, delete your text or go to the origin URL
  • Quick copy text from your MultiClipboard by clicking the clipped text
  • Quick access the origin URL by clicking the screenshot, next to your clipped text
